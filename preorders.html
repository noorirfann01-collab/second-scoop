How Pre-Orders Work

We bake in small batches and drop in limited windows. Here's how to never miss one.

1

A drop opens

We announce a batch. The bar at the top of the site shows the live status and deadline.

2

You pre-order

Add pre-order items to your cart and check out as normal. You'll get an order number instantly.

3

The window closes

Once the deadline hits, we lock the batch and start baking. No late orders — that's what keeps it fresh.

4

Pickup or delivery

We fulfil on the drop date you chose, by pickup or delivery, and confirm timing with you directly.

Product statuses, explained

Every product shows one of these statuses so you always know where things stand:

Pre-Order Buy now, fulfilled on the next drop date.
Closing Soon The pre-order window is about to shut.
Coming Soon Teased now, not yet orderable.
Sold Out This batch is gone — watch for a restock.
